Welcome to my Grade 4 story! Grade 4 was an amazing year! I learned so much, tried new things, and had fun along the way. Here's a look at everything I accomplished.

## 📚 Books I Read

### Basic English Grammar for Dummies
*By Willies Publication*

My first proper textbook! This was my first time reading a textbook cover to cover. I learned grammar fundamentals that help me write better.

### Creative Problem Solving in School Mathematics
*By George Lencher*

An amazing math book that taught me creative problem-solving strategies. The puzzles were challenging but so satisfying to solve!

## 💻 Coding Journey

**Python Programming:** Mastered the basics!
- Data types: numbers, strings, lists, sets, dictionaries
- Conditionals and loops
- Functions and reusable code

**LeetCode:** Solved 140+ problems! Regular practice helps me think logically and learn new algorithms.

**First Game:** Built a Snake Game! Used Python and Pygame to create a playable game that runs in the browser.

## 🧮 Math & Competitions

**Kangaroo Math Competition:** Scored 76/120

This was my first competitive math exam! I learned that:
- Practice tests don't always predict real test performance
- Test-taking is a skill that takes practice
- One score doesn't define my abilities

*Important lesson: Exams can be tricky even when you're well prepared. I'm proud of myself for trying!*

## 🎹 Music & Other Interests

- **Piano:** Continued playing and practicing - achieved RCM Level 2!
- **Art:** Explored creative projects
- **Problem Solving:** Always looking for new challenges

## 💭 What Grade 4 Taught Me

- Hard work pays off - whether it's coding, math, or piano
- It's okay to not get everything right the first time
- Learning is a journey, not a destination
- Trying new things (like competitions) helps me grow
- Balancing different interests makes life interesting!

## 🚀 Ready for Grade 5!

Grade 4 was amazing, but I'm excited for what's next! Grade 5 brings new challenges, new things to learn, and more opportunities to grow.
